### Unit 1: Proofs

Proof: A _mathematical proof_ of a _proposition_ is a chain of logical deductions leading to the proposition from a base set of _axioms_.

#### Inferences
*modus ponens*: a proof of P + proof that P implies Q is a proof of Q
